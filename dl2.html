Step into a world as Aiden Caldwell, a skilled wanderer with a mysterious past,
entering The City, one of the last human settlements torn apart by conflict and infected by a deadly virus.
Scavenge through crumbling buildings, craft powerful tools and weapons, and shape the fate of a city where every choice matters.
Master agile parkour to scale rooftops and evade enemies, or dive into visceral melee combat against hostile survivors and evolving infected.
By day, explore a vibrant world full of danger and opportunity. By night, survive relentless terrors that lurk in the shadows.
In this open-world action RPG, your decisions determine the balance of power and the lives of those around you.
Will you cling to your humanity or let the darkness in?

Dying Light

Survive the night. Fight the infected. Dying Light is the ultimate zombie parkour experience.
